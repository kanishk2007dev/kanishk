About Kanishk Mahawar

Kanishk Mahawar is the founder of 7 Pairs Digital, a modern media and entertainment company redefining digital creativity. With a passion for music and storytelling, he has launched the popular podcast Melody Minutes, where he shares insights, music, and conversations with emerging artists. His journey from a young creator to a brand innovator highlights his vision for the future of entertainment.

About 7 Pairs Digital

Founded in 2025, 7 Pairs Digital is a creative company focused on digital media, branding, and music production. Originally known as KDEC, it was rebranded to reflect a broader mission – connecting ideas and innovation in pairs, across seven key creative fields. The company’s mission is to empower creators with the tools, platforms, and strategies they need to succeed.

Melody Minutes Podcast

Melody Minutes is an engaging podcast hosted by Kanishk Mahawar that explores the art of music, creativity, and inspiration. With episodes featuring new sounds, artist interviews, and behind-the-scenes stories, it has become a must-listen for music enthusiasts.
