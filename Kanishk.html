A Thoughtful Rebrand & Historical Foundation

KDEC (Krop Digital Media Entertainment Company) has officially been rebranded as 7 Pairs Digital. The rebrand embodies the evolution and growth of a venture rooted in creativity. Originally conceived to launch podcasts and web-based storytelling, the company quickly evolved—expanding into music production, digital content creation, and multi-platform narrative design. The new identity not only reflects the company’s successful journey so far but also signals its next phase: an ambitious expansion into modern digital entertainment under a fresh and memorable name.

Kanishk Mahawar, the company's founder, explains: “The name 7 Pairs Digital captures the essence of duality, collaboration, and innovation—core values that drive our creative vision.” This rebranding is not a break from the past, but a refinement—embracing its foundational achievements while preparing the company for broader creative horizons.
